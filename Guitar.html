The guitar (in its standard tuning and string count) is a 6 stringed guitar that has a range of e2 to e6 it is the primary instrument of many music generas that emerged in the 1940's to the early 2010's the job of the guitar player is to fill the midrange and melody of the band as well as the primary source of sound next to the vocals in the music

Cort

Cort is a great brand for beginners or people looking to get into the hobby but don't want to invest a huge amount of money but also don't want to cheap out and get a bad product.

ESP

ESP is a Brand that offers many guitars that mimic the shapes of models from other brands but with hardware better suited for metal and extreme metal

Fender

Fender/Squire is best known for its classic and recognisable designs. Their guitars generally have a crisp, clear and raw sound. They are best suited to genres like Punk, Classic rock, Blues, Country and anything clean. The Squire line of guitars have the same models as the others but at a reduced price if you don’t want to spend a huge amount of money getting into the hobby

Gibson

Gibson/Epiphone are a very versatile brand with models for all genres. The les paul and les paul jr are great for hard rock, lighter metal genres and punk, the SGs are great for metal and hard rock, and firebird explorer and flying v are great for metal and some of the extreme metal genres. These guitars are very high price but high value The Epiphone line of guitars have the same models as the others but at a reduced price if you don't want to spend a huge amount of money getting into the hobby buy epiphone still make great guitars that are more costly than the other cheaper brands like Squire and Cort.

Ibanez

Ibanez is best known for its unique finishes and Smooth cleans and heavy distortion tone. Ibanez also makes guitar models with various amounts of strings from the normal 6 to 9 string electrics. Ibanez is best suited for Metal, Extreme metal, Punk and hard rock. The GIO line of guitars have the same models as the others but at a reduced price if you don’t want to spend a huge amount of money getting into the hobby

Music man

Music man offers a range of guitars with unique but comfortable shapes. The tones that these guitars produce are best for lead, metal and hard rock
